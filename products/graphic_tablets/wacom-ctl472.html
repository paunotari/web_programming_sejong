Wacom CTL-472

The Wacom CTL-472 is a versatile drawing tablet that brings precision, natural pen pressure, and intuitive controls, making it the perfect tool for digital artists and creators of all levels.

2048 Levels of Pressure Sensitivity
Compact, Lightweight Design
USB Connectivity
ExpressKeys for Shortcuts

Connectivity & Controls

Connects via USB; includes two customizable ExpressKeys for quick shortcuts and a responsive drawing surface.

Design

Compact and slim design, ideal for small workspaces and easy portability.

Performance

Offers 2048 levels of pressure sensitivity for precise, natural drawing and fine detail control.

Customization

Two ExpressKeys allow you to personalize your shortcuts for a more efficient workflow.

Compatibility

Compatible with Windows, macOS, and Android devices, working seamlessly with most creative software.

Unleash your creativity.

Whether you're sketching, editing photos, or designing, the Wacom CTL-472 offers a smooth and responsive experience that brings your creative ideas to life.
