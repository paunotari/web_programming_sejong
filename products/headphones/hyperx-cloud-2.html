HyperX Cloud II

The HyperX Cloud II is a legendary gaming headset known for its comfort, durability, and powerful virtual 7.1 surround sound, making it a favorite among gamers worldwide.

Virtual 7.1 Surround Sound
53mm Drivers
Detachable Noise-Canceling Mic
Durable Aluminum Frame

Connectivity & Controls

USB sound card for PC/Mac and 3.5mm jack for consoles; onboard volume, mic, and surround controls for quick adjustments.

Design

Classic closed-back style with memory foam ear cushions and leatherette headband for long-session comfort.

Performance

Immersive 7.1 surround sound and powerful 53mm drivers deliver crystal-clear audio and deep bass.

Build Quality

Built with a solid aluminum frame and detachable parts to withstand daily wear and travel.

Compatibility

Works with PC, PS4/PS5, Xbox (via 3.5mm), Nintendo Switch, and mobile devices.

Hear the difference.

Whether you're gaming, working, or streaming, the HyperX Cloud II brings premium sound and all-day comfort to any setup.
