Archer T3U Plus

Full HD 1080p webcam with auto-light balance, integrated privacy shutter, and built-in mic

USB 3.0
WEP, WPA/WPA2, WPA-PSK/WPA2-PSK
Certifications FCC, CE, RoHS

Adjustable, Multi-Directional Antenna

Rotate and adjust the multi-directional antenna to improve user experience and performance in different environments.

Speed

The Archer T3U Plus receives Wi-Fi signals on two separate bands for all your online needs. Choose the 2.4 GHz band for up to 400 Mbps of surfing, emailing, and social media, or switch to 5 GHz for up to 867 Mbps of gaming, HD streaming, and large file downloads.

Connection

With two high-gain antennas built in one, Archer T3U Plus boosts your signal reception and data transmission much significantly than traditional single-antenna WiFi adapter. It means greater WiFi coverage and more stable WiFi connection without WiFi drops and lag, even from father away or across multiple walls.

Range

The advanced serial high-gain antenna structure creates a better overall connection throughout your home.

Fast, Reliable Wi-Fi for Any Setup

The TP-Link Archer T3U Plus is a dual-band Wi-Fi adapter that supports high-speed IEEE 802.11ac connectivity, perfect for desktops or notebooks. With its adjustable high-gain antenna, it ensures stronger signal reception and stable wireless performance, whether for streaming, gaming, or everyday browsing.
