Asus USB-AC53 Nano Dual-Band Wireless Adapter

The Asus USB-AC53 Nano is a high-speed dual-band USB Wi-Fi adapter built for compact, seamless connectivity. Whether you're upgrading your PC or laptop, it delivers a stable, fast wireless experience without bulk or hassle.

Dual-band 802.11ac Wi-Fi
Up to 867 Mbps on 5GHz
USB 2.0 plug-and-play
Ultra-compact nano design

Main Feauters

Delivers up to 867 Mbps on the 5GHz band and 300 Mbps on 2.4GHz, with support for MU-MIMO technology and easy setup. It’s ideal for streaming, gaming, or everyday browsing.

Design

Ultra-compact "nano" form factor designed to be left in your device without protruding—ideal for portability and tight setups.

Speed

Dual-band AC Wi-Fi: up to 867 Mbps (5GHz) and 300 Mbps (2.4GHz), providing smooth HD streaming and fast downloads.

Build Quality

Minimal yet durable construction with a low-profile plug, built to be travel-friendly and long-lasting under constant use.

Compatibility

Works with Windows 10/8.1/8/7 and macOS. Backward compatible with 802.11a/b/g/n networks.
