Keychron K8 Pro

The Keychron K8 Pro is a wireless mechanical keyboard built for precision, comfort, and customization, featuring QMK/VIA support and hot-swappable switches for a truly personal typing experience.

QMK/VIA Programmable
Hot-Swappable Switches
Gasket-Mounted Design
Bluetooth 5.1 & USB-C

Connectivity & Ports

Supports Bluetooth 5.1 for wireless use, or USB-C for wired; compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux with OS toggle switch.

Design

Compact tenkeyless layout with CNC aluminum frame and per-key RGB lighting.

Performance

Responsive keystrokes with minimal latency, ideal for typing or gaming.

Build Quality

Gasket-mounted for a softer feel, with durable materials that last.

Compatibility

Fully compatible with macOS, Windows, and Linux; keycaps included for both layouts.

Customize your keystrokes.

With full QMK/VIA support, hot-swappable switches, and flexible connectivity, the K8 Pro is built to match your workflow or playstyle exactly how you want.
